Battery Pack Modeling

Open Battery Pack Model
This model includes two variants of Battery Pack (1. One module with 6 cells in series, 2. 16 modules with 96 cells in total). Battery Pack is connected to Charger / Load via Precharge Circuit and contactors. Precharge circuit is required ensure there is no sudden rush of current in/out of battery pack when connected to Charger or Load with large voltage difference. Closing or opening precharge contactors is done by BMS.
